Rhossili Bay is three miles of Atlantic beach at the western tip of the Gower Peninsula, dog-friendly all year and one of the longer open beaches in Wales. Dog walks on the Gower extend from the beach to the Down ridge directly above it, with open grassland and headland walking that suits dogs well. The peninsula rewards a full day.
