The Gower Peninsula was the first place in Britain designated an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty. Rhossili Bay is the headline - three miles of unrestricted dog beach at the tip of the peninsula - but the Down ridge above it and the hidden Victorian woodland at Penllergare are both worth the detour. A full day combining two Rhossili walks is one of the better days out with a dog in Wales.
