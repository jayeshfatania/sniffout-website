Perthshire sits at the meeting point of Highland and Lowland Scotland, and the walks reflect that. The Hermitage at Dunkeld puts you beneath some of the tallest trees in Britain beside a dramatic river gorge. Kinnoull Hill above Perth is one of the finest accessible viewpoints in Scotland for the effort it asks. Both are well suited to dogs and manageable in a half day.
