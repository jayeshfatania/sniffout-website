Loch Lomond is the largest body of fresh water in Great Britain and one of the most accessible stretches of wild Scotland from Glasgow. The eastern shore at Balmaha is a West Highland Way waypoint and gives a straightforward lochside walk with swimming for dogs. Arrive early in summer - the car park fills by mid-morning on fine weekends.
