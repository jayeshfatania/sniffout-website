Dog walks in the Peak District range from the gentle limestone valleys of the White Peak to the wild gritstone moorland of the Dark Peak. Dovedale, Mam Tor, Stanage Edge, and Kinder Scout between them cover most of what makes this national park worth visiting on foot.
