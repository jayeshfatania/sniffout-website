Grasmere is one of the gentler ways into the Lake District, with lakeside paths and valley meadows rather than fell scrambles. The fells elsewhere in the park are worth knowing about for the future, but they ask more of a dog than many are ready for. Sheep are everywhere up here. Keep the lead close, and watch the forecast - weather comes in fast.
