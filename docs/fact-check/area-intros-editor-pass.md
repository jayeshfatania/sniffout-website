## Overall Pattern Note

Six of the eight intros use a space-hyphen-space construction (" - ") as a substitute for an em dash. This is a systemic error across the batch, not isolated mistakes. The rule is no em dashes in user-facing copy; the " - " construction is functionally identical to an em dash and fails the same test. Every instance has been flagged and a corrected construction provided below.

Three intros also contain factual errors that contradict the published walk pages. These are marked CRITICAL and must be corrected before Developer brief.

Dogs need to be on leads in Dockey Wood during bluebell season and near the sheep on Ivinghoe Hills year-round.
