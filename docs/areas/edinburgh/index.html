Dog walks in Edinburgh

Dog walks in Edinburgh - volcanic hills, woodland paths, and open moorland within the city. Off-lead and terrain details for every route.

Edinburgh has more off-lead space within its boundaries than almost any other city in Britain. Arthur’s Seat and Holyrood Park sit a short walk from the centre, while the Pentland Hills begin just seven miles south. The city rewards early starts - the hills are quieter before 9am and the views are better for it.
